Final Thoughts


– We added a PayPal button to the new donation landing page which we believe will increase donation rates.

– We moved the statistics to the top of our website to give more hierarchy to the positive track-record of Make-A-Wish.

– With our new simplified navigation I believe we made the website easier to use.

– When we centered the donation page we made it more task-oriented for users rather than distracting like Make-A-Wish’s current stylistic donation page.

– We decided to keep the same color scheme as current on the website to keep branding consistent.

– The way we did a combination of a hamburger and top menu navigation made navigating links simpler and users preferred it in A/B testing.

– Overall the team believes in the mission behind Make-A-Wish’s website and hopes the iterations we came up with can improve the success of their non-profit.
